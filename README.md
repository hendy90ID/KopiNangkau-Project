## <p align='center'>KopiNangKau Overview</p>

<p>&emsp;Based on sales chart year-to-year, I found 2 critical findings: decrease in sales volume and increase in total revenue. Decrease in sales volume can be described as follow: <br>
Americano (2021) total 13.634 units --> Cappuccino (2022) total 13.411 units --> Espresso (2023) total 13.319 units. <br>
It indicating a decrease of a total of 315 units (2.31%) of overall sales volume.<br>
For total revenue rose up by 0.65% in second year and 0.89% in third year. It is clearly not healthy comparing towards inflation rate of current countries where the business located (Indonesia).<br>
Food and beverage ratio in a whole are 15.93% and 84.07% respectively. It is common in a coffeshop where it can create an opportunity for further improvements.
</p>
Solution : 
<p>
 1. <b>Online Marketing</b><br>
  &emsp; Registering business into online food marketplace such as Grab or Shopeefood to boost business awareness, hiring social media influencer to promote the business, and increase customer retention program such as membership promotions on the tenth purchases to get discount or free products.
</p>
<p>
 2. <b>Food Conversion Campaign</b><br>
  &emsp; Based on the food and beverage ratio, the room of development on food sector is still wide open. The business owner can either adjust budget allocation towards food sector or cross selling promotion on beverages towards food with a discount.
</p>
